Deepfake Detection Research

Title

Research in deepfake detection for video and audio spoofing scenarios.

Objective

Improve model robustness and detection quality under real-world conditions such as compression artifacts and scene changes.

Process

Researched CNN/LSTM-based models, processed large datasets (~500GB), used GradCAM and network visualization for model diagnostics, and refined training data based on observed failure patterns.

Tools

PyTorch, TensorFlow, Keras, OpenCV, GradCAM, large-scale data pipelines.

Value Proposition

Highlights research rigor and production-minded problem solving, including published results and performance improvements in real applications.
